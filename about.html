Our Story - Kenya's Printing & Branding Leader

Meet Our CEO

King'ori Trey Mwangi

Founder & Chief Executive Officer - buni Brands Kenya

"Great branding isn't just about looking good—it's about communicating value, building trust, and creating lasting connections with your audience across Kenya."

Trey Mwangi is the CEO and Founder of buni Brands Kenya. He is the creative mind behind the company's growth into one of the most respected branding agencies in the region, serving clients throughout Kenya.


With over a decade of experience in design, print, and visual strategy, Trey has led buni Brands to multiple awards and industry recognition across Machakos, Nairobi, and beyond.


His approach combines business insight with design thinking ensuring every project is not only visually impactful but also strategically sound for Kenyan market success.


Known for his precision, creative vision, and relentless commitment to quality, he has positioned buni Brands as a trusted partner for forward-thinking organizations in Kenya.


Trey remains closely involved in both creative direction and client engagement, pushing the team to exceed expectations and deliver work that speaks for itself across the Kenyan market.

Who We Are

buni Brands is an award-winning creative branding and printing agency with several branches across Kenya. Recognized multiple times as the leading branding company in the region, we continue to set the standard for excellence in design, print, and visual communication for businesses in Nairobi and beyond.


We specialize in delivering high-quality design, print, and brand solutions for businesses, institutions, and individuals across Kenya. Our comprehensive services help clients establish strong market presence through professional branding.


Our core focus is to help Kenyan clients establish a strong visual identity and market presence through professional branding services. From concept development to final production, we provide a full range of solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of each client in Nairobi and throughout Kenya.


We are committed to creativity, quality, reliability, and excellent customer service. At buni Brands Kenya, we believe that branding is more than just visuals; it is about making lasting impressions that drive business growth.


Our team comprises experienced designers, branding experts, and production specialists who work together to ensure that every project is executed with precision and purpose for clients across Kenya.
