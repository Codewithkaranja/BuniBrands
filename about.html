Our Story

Who We Are

Buni Brands is an award-winning creative branding and printing agency with several branches in Kenya. Recognized multiple times as the leading branding company in the region, we continue to set the standard for excellence in design, print, and visual communication.


We specialize in delivering high-quality design, print, and brand solutions for businesses, institutions, and individuals.


Our core focus is to help our clients establish a strong visual identity and market presence through professional branding services. From concept development to final production, we provide a full range of solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of each client.


We are committed to creativity, quality, reliability, and excellent customer service. At Buni Brands, we believe that branding is more than just visuals; it is about making lasting impressions.


Our team comprises experienced designers, branding experts, and production specialists who work together to ensure that every project is executed with precision and purpose.

Meet Our CEO

Peter Mwangi

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

"Great branding isn't just about looking good—it's about communicating value, building trust, and creating lasting connections with your audience."

Peter Mwangi is the CEO and Founder of Buni Brands. He is the creative mind behind the company’s growth into one of the most respected branding agencies in the region.


With over a decade of experience in design, print, and visual strategy, Peter has led Buni Brands to multiple awards and industry recognition across Machakos and beyond.


His approach combines business insight with design thinking ensuring every project is not only visually impactful but also strategically sound. Known for his precision, creative vision, and relentless commitment to quality, he has positioned Buni Brands as a trusted partner for forward-thinking organizations.


Peter remains closely involved in both creative direction and client engagement. His leadership continues to push the team to exceed expectations and deliver work that speaks for itself.
