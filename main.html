About Buni Brands

Our Story

Founded over a decade ago, Buni Brands has grown from a small printing shop to Kenya's leading branding and printing agency. Our journey began with a simple vision: to help businesses communicate their unique stories through exceptional visual branding.

Today, we serve clients across various industries, delivering innovative solutions that combine creativity with cutting-edge technology.

Meet Our CEO

Peter Mwangi

With over 15 years of experience in the branding and printing industry, Peter Mwangi has been the driving force behind Buni Brands' success. His visionary leadership and commitment to excellence have positioned the company as a market leader.

Peter believes in the power of visual communication to transform businesses and is passionate about helping clients achieve their branding goals through innovative solutions.
